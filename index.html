About Me
My name is Erick, and I am an undergrad who attends UCR. Although I am currently undeclared, I plan to major in History and minor in Education. My goal is to go into the education field and become a high school history teacher.

I want to go into the education field due to my want to give back to my community, as I believe that grassroots level actions of people and organizations are key to help the people in those communities facing difficulties.
